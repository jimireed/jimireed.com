AI Growth Alliance

Aligning regional talent to ensure Hampshire & the Solent leads on AI adoption

The AI Growth Alliance (AIGA) is a regional partnership bringing together business, education, government and civic leaders across Hampshire, the Solent and the wider South Central region. Its role is to align effort, reduce fragmentation, and make it easier for organisations to adopt AI confidently and responsibly.

Working towards a formal launch at the Farnborough International Airshow in July 2026, the Alliance has formed four working groups: AI Skills & Leadership, AI Infrastructure & Investment, AI Ethics & Inclusion, and AI Ecosystem Coordination.

As a member, I contribute to the Alliance's mission of ensuring the South does not simply observe the AI revolution — but helps shape it.

"AI-driven growth cannot be delivered by data centres alone. It will come from our people understanding how to deploy AI responsibly, from our SMEs being confident enough to adopt it, and from our institutions being able to apply it in real-world industrial sectors across our region."

David Patterson — Chair, AI Growth Alliance
